Spraac

Spraac is a mobile application that assists soon-to-be social professionals to practise and master their conversation techniques. Making practise material accessible to this target group through video calling, and providing students with semi-realistic roleplaying instructions, their study success is maximized.

This app was developed in cooperation with Hanze University's School of Social Studies, and was developed as part of my bachelor's project - which was graded with a 9.2 and was nominated for the CMD Thesis Award 2019. Following my bachelor's, I was hired on a freelance basis by the Hanze to further develop this product in the Blackboard environment, one that suited their needs better.

Check out my Github for the project's sourcecode, or click here for my complete thesis.

Postzegels Please

Do YOU like order and structure? Do you like to organise? Do you like mail?! Well, go ahead and download Postzegels Please and give us a hand sorting colourful packages and letters. Only YOU can ensure that the citizens of Postland will successfully receive their mail. But watch out! You should shred the contraband or else it might explode. Good luck, postman!

Postzegels Please is the game we developed during the Global Game Jam 2021, an 48-hour hackathon-like event during which you completely design and develop a game that fits a theme. Together with four other young-professionals we built this game using Unity3D, and it's source code can be found here.

IntoReality

IntoReality is a video-editing tool that allows you to add interactive elements to your 360-degree video content. This application was developed in cooperation with Hanze University as an educational tool. Adding interactive elements increases the educational value of the video experience while making it more entertaining as well. While we were developing this tool, Hanze University was expanding their education to include 360-degree video content for their social studies as a means to prepare them for real-life scenario's.

During this project, i've been closesely involved with the design process, and fulfilled the position of lead software engineer. The sourcecode for this project can be found at my Github.

Samenwerkingsspel

Samenwerkingsspel (Cooperation Game) is an applied game we developed during my internship at 8D Games. This game is part of a larger project to help officials reduce the time needed to grant/deny permits. At the time, the speed at which permit requests would be processed was very slow due to the lack of communication between different parties.

This game focusses on the verbal communication and challenges its players to construct a well working rocket, that meets their own demands and the demands of their entire team. Together with colleagues, I was responsible for the design and development of this program.
