Resume

I am a software engineer, graduated from from the Hanze University of Applied Sciences (Hanze UAS) and am currently pursuing a MSc. in Computing Science at the University of Groningen (RUG). Being the first person ever to acquire this combination of degrees, I attempt to combine User Centered Design and Software Engineering principles in order to create high quality software that fit the users' needs as well as possible. In my design philosophy, I consider the client to be co-captain together with the development on their projects, leading us into a direction that complements their needs and is evaluated with our professional experience.
