Spraac

Spraac is a mobile application that assists students in social studies to master their conversation techniques.
By making practicing accessible through video calling, and providing the students with a semi-realistic roleplaying instructions, their study success is maximized.
In cooperation with Hanze University's School of Social Studies, I developed Spraac as my bachelor project, with which I was nominated for that year's thesis award.

IntoReality

IntoReality is a video-editing tool that allows you to add interactive elements to your 360-degree video content. This application was developed in cooperation with Hanze University as an educational tool.
Adding interactive elements increases the educational value of the video experience while making it more entertaining as well.
Besides being closely involved in the entire design process, I fulfilled the position of lead software engineer in this project.

Samenwerkingsspel

Samenwerkingsspel (Cooperation Game) is an applied game we developed during my internship as game developer at 8D Games.
This game is part of a larger plan to help officials reduce the time needed to grant/deny permits.
This game focusses on the verbal communication and challenges its players to constuct a well working rocket, that meets their personal demands and the demands of their entire team.
